PROJECTS & USEFUL EXPERIENCE

BlackJack Game With AI - C#

Recreated the popular betting game into a console, text-based simulator with a smart AI that maximizes its odds of winning. Allows for up to 8 players, human or computer, to challenge the dealer I created two levels of AI for this game. The first follows conventional rules for when to hit, double, or bust, based on its hand and the dealer's up-card. The second meticulously considers all possible hand combinations with respect to the remaining cards in the deck. Based on this, it determines the likelihood of each possible outcome for the dealer's hand and generates its ratio of winning if it passes or hits. Implemented data structures such as lists to represent the card deck, enumerated types, and inheritance.

Pokemon Generation 1 Battle Simulator - C#

Driven by nostalgia to create a console based representation of the original Pokemon game. Extensively followed generation 1 battle mechanics to replicate battle conditions in Pokemon Red/Blue/Yellow. Utilized abstraction, inheritance, and intricate control flow to create over 50 unique Pokemon and 100 moves. Features include Pokemon dual types, move types, status impairments (poisoned, paralyzed, etc.), multi-move attacks, and temporary stat multipliers. Players can battle with up to 6 Pokemon each, which can be personally selected or assigned randomly.

"StressLess Dining Chair" for Children With ADHD

With my group of 4 other team members, went through the entire design process to prototype and build a functional chair aimed to alleviate family stress by reducing potential outbursts of children with ADHD. ADHD is a particularly devastating mental disease for a child because it can directly affect everyone surrounding it. Consequently, my group sought to provide a solution at the dinner table, often symbolic of a harmonic family, to promote social interacitons between the afflicted child and its family. During this process, we collaboratively worked through multiple concept generations to create an ergonomic product.
